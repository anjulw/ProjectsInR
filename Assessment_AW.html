Aim of the Report

In this report ebola labratory-confirmed cases in Liberia, Sierra Leone, the United Kingdom and Spain will be investigated for the years 2014 - 2016. The ebola data set is downloaded form the Projects in R Repository.

Labratory-confirmed Cases over the Years

Year 2014

Please note that the data collection only started in August 2014. In Figure 1 the development from end of August 2014 to December 2014 is displayed. A rapid increase in cases in Sierra Leone and a slight increase in Liberia and no increase in Spain is observable. For the United Kingdome no ebola infection was reported during this time period.

Conclusion

In this report it can be obseverd that after an ebola outbreak (>1), there is an rapide increase in labratory-confirmed cases which stabilizes over the following years. It should be the main aim to prevent an ebola outbrake at any cost.
